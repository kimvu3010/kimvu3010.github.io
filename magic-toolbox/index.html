Magic Toolbox Add-on

Welcome to Magic Toolbox, a powerful add-on for Google Sheets that makes it easy to manage and organize your data.

With Magic Toolbox, you can:

  • Sort and filter your data to find exactly what you need
  • Automatically format your data for better readability
  • Create custom charts and visualizations to better understand your data

Get started today by installing Magic Toolbox on your Google Sheets account!
